Welcome to King Mixer - Your Intelligent Mixing Assistant

King Mixer is a VST3 channel strip plugin designed to give you
professional-quality mix starting points in seconds. Simply select
your genre and instrument, click Apply Rule, and King Mixer sets
all the key parameters to match industry-standard mixing practices.

WHAT'S INCLUDED:

  - 4-Band Parametric EQ (Low Shelf, Low-Mid, High-Mid, High Shelf)
  - Compressor with Makeup Gain
  - Saturation (analog-style tanh waveshaper)
  - Stereo Width Control (mid-side processing)
  - Reverb Send
  - Input / Output Gain Staging

SUPPORTED GENRES:

  Rock, Pop, Hip-Hop, EDM, Jazz, R&B, Metal, Classical

SUPPORTED INSTRUMENTS:

  Vocals, Drums, Bass, Electric Guitar, Acoustic Guitar,
  Keys/Synths, Strings, Brass

HOW TO USE:

  1. Load King Mixer as a VST3 plugin on any track in your DAW
  2. Select the Genre and Instrument from the dropdowns
  3. Click "Apply Rule" to load recommended settings
  4. Fine-tune any knob to taste
  5. Use the Mix knob to blend between dry and processed signal

64 curated mix presets (8 genres x 8 instruments) are built in,
each one crafted from professional mixing best practices.

No external dependencies required. Just install and go.
